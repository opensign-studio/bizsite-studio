# Style Pack — Boutique Editorial

The original Open Sign look. Elegant, feminine, refined; high-contrast serif on
warm cream with a metallic accent. Reference build: tryst-salon.vercel.app.

## Movement

Quiet luxury of a neighborhood boutique. Cream paper (never pure white), one
ink, one feature accent sampled from the business, one metallic (brass) for
kickers and stars. High-contrast serif display (Cormorant Garamond) + light
geometric sans (Jost). Generous letterspacing on uppercase labels.

## Navigation

Sticky translucent **top bar** with backdrop blur; shadow only after scroll.
Wordmark left (letterspaced display serif = the logo), uppercase nav center/right,
phone **pill button** right. Hamburger → slide-down panel ≤720px.

## Hero archetype — arch + badge

Split. Left: status chip → h1 with an italic accent phrase → one-paragraph lede →
two CTAs (call = primary pill, secondary ghost) → fact row over a hairline.
Right: best interior photo in an **arch** (`border-radius:999px 999px R R`,
`aspect-ratio:4/5`, heavy soft shadow) + a floating white rating-badge card.
Photo column `order:-1` on mobile.

## Section paradigm — card-based, alternating bands

One page, in order:
1. Ribbon: ink background, row of uppercase micro-items with small line icons.
2. About: overlapping photo pair (main 4:3 + inset square, thick cream border)
   opposite kicker + title + lede + em-dash list.
3. Services: **3×2 white cards** — tinted rounded icon square (inline SVG line
   icons), serif h3, two-line description; hover `translateY(-5px)` + deeper
   shadow. End with a "call for pricing" note.
4. Gallery: uniform square grid (3→2→1), hover zoom + gradient overlay, lightbox.
5. Feature/boutique: ink band, copy + tag pills left, 2×2 photo collage right.
6. Reviews: 3 white cards, oversized serif open-quote, metallic stars, real quotes.
7. Visit: hours card (today-row highlighted) + contact card side by side; full-width
   ~380px map band below.
8. Footer: ink; wordmark + blurb, visit + hours columns, back-to-top.

## Grid & interaction

Symmetric, contained (~1140px). Rounded corners (radius ~14–22px) and soft
shadows are on-brand *here* (the one pack where uniform rounding is intentional —
it reads boutique). Reveal-on-scroll fade-up.

## Type & color logic

Serif display 500–700 + italic accent; sans body 300. Palette: ink (darkest
interior tone), accent (feature wall/awning), cream bg, brass for luxe accents,
optional deep contrast (wine). Dark sections use ink; light sections alternate
cream gradients.
